About Whisk.ly

What is Whisk.ly?

Whisk.ly started as the brain-child of Breland, a software development engineer with a passion for food.

Looking for a better way to store and view recipes, Breland teamed up with George to create a product that was both functional and beautiful, with a focus on sharing and privacy.

Designed with a recipe first in mind, Whisk.ly creates an unique cooking expirience by using Recipe Stories, built-in shopping lists, device integration, text-to-speech and more.

Features

At Whisk.ly we pride ourself on creating one of the most advanced cooking app out there. Below are the features we felt most important to have in our pubic launch:

  • Multi-device login
  • Recipe stories
  • Sharing and embedding recipes
  • Shopping lists
  • Timer
  • Recipe read-out
  • Custom yields

Future Features

We are constantly trying to make Whisk.ly a better product. In doing so we have laid out features we are working on including in future releases.

It's important to us that Whisk.ly has the most useful features for you while you prep your favorite dish. Have an idea for new features? Send us an email or tweet.

  • Amazon Echo integration
  • Amazon Fresh integration
  • OCR recipe recognition
  • Smart devices

Open source, for real

We pride ourself for creating and maintaining an open source app.

Whisk.ly was created using mainly open source solutions so it only seems right that Whisk.ly be open source as well.

Interested in how a recipe is stored or a shopping list is built? Check out Whisk.ly on GitHub.
